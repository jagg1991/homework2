My first big trip!

On my first big journey accross the ocean, I went to London. It was one of the best expeience of my life, and one that I will charise forever. I can still hear Big Ben's iconic bell as if I was still there. The cleanliness of the city still amazes me for how big the population is.


Paris!

My next big trip was to Paris. It really holds up it's name for city of love! I fell in love with the food, and getting to know some of the Parisians. The thing I loved most about the city is how old the city felt, yet have a modern touch to it. Seeing the char marks from the bombing of World War 2 made feel as if I was walking through histroy.

Aloha!!
